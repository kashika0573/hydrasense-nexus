Drought Education

What is drought?

Drought happens when a region has less water than usual for a long period of time. It can be caused by low rainfall, reduced snowpack, high heat, or low reservoir storage.

💡 Science Drop

Snowpack acts like a natural water bank. When mountain snow melts, it slowly feeds rivers and reservoirs. Less snowpack can mean less stored water later.
